## Full description

HomeGentic puts your home's maintenance history on the blockchain — permanently verified, privately owned, and ready when you need it most.

**For homeowners**
Log every repair, renovation, and service visit. Upload receipts, permits, and photos. Get a HomeGentic Score that reflects how well-maintained your home really is — and share a verified report with buyers when it's time to sell.

- Log jobs in seconds via voice or form
- Get a verified HomeFax Score (0–100)
- Predict which systems need attention next
- Share tamper-proof reports with buyers and agents
- Sign off on contractor work directly from your phone

**For contractors**
Browse open quote requests in your trades, submit bids, and build a verified work history that homeowners can trust.

- See leads matched to your specialties
- Submit bids with one tap
- Get notified when homeowners accept or respond
- Build a trust score backed by real verified jobs

**Your data, your chain**
Records are stored on the Internet Computer blockchain — a public network you own, not a private server we control. No vendor lock-in. No data brokering.

**Privacy first**
Login uses Internet Identity — a cryptographic key, not your email or phone number. No password to forget, no personal info to leak.
